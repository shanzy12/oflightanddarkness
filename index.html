Of Light and Darkness

D&D by Ethan Mick

A campaign set in the Isles of Mirth in the "New World." Explorers DJ, Edis, Gavyn, and Sora travelled on a Red Sunrise Trading Company ship from Casera in search of rivers of gold. Upon arrival, they found a curious world (and no rivers). They met a fifth party member, Phelista, on the island Dellomar.
